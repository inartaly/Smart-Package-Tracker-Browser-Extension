# Smart Package Tracker — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

Smart Package Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality happens locally on your device.

---

## 📦 What the extension does
- Adds a right‑click option to track a selected package number.
- Detects the carrier (UPS, USPS, FedEx) based on the selected text.
- Copies the selected tracking number to your clipboard.
- Opens a new tab to the appropriate carrier’s tracking page.

All processing occurs **locally** in your browser.

---

## 🔒 What the extension does *not* do
- ❌ No data collection  
- ❌ No analytics  
- ❌ No tracking  
- ❌ No cookies  
- ❌ No remote code execution  
- ❌ No communication with external servers (other than opening the carrier’s public tracking page)

The extension does not read or modify any webpage content beyond the text you manually select.

---

## 🛠️ Permissions Explanation

### `contextMenus`
Used to add the “Track Package” option when you right‑click selected text.

### `clipboardWrite`
Used to copy the selected tracking number to your clipboard.

### `tabs`
Used to open a new tab with the carrier’s tracking page.

### `scripting`
Used only to run a small script that copies the selected text to your clipboard.

### `host_permissions: <all_urls>`
Required by Microsoft Edge to allow clipboard operations on all pages.  
No page content is read, stored, or transmitted.

---

## 🌐 Third‑party services
The extension opens the following public tracking pages:

- UPS  
- USPS  
- FedEx  

No data is sent anywhere else.
